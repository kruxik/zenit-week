# App Naming & Brand — Zenit

## Problem Statement
How Might We name a week-planning tool that communicates focus, intentionality, and anti-burnout — for senior knowledge workers (CEOs, CTOs, VPs) and broader public — in both English and Czech?

## Recommended Direction

**Name: Zenit** (stylised as **Zen**•it)
**Full product name: Zenit Week**

Three-layer meaning embedded in one word:
1. **Zenith** — the peak, where you want your week to be
2. **Zen** — mental clarity and calm; the emotional outcome the app delivers
3. **it** — action, doing, "just do it"; the weekly planning behaviour

This triple meaning is rare and on-brand. The Jar of Life philosophy IS Zen thinking applied to time — protect the big rocks, let the rest go, feel at peace with your week.

**Visual identity requirement:** The Zen/it split must be visible in the logotype — `Zen` in a softer weight or colour, `it` in bold or contrasting style. Without this the pun is invisible and you just have a misspelled "zenith."

**Brand tone:** Productivity tool with Zen philosophy — NOT a meditation app. Sharp, minimal, structured. Avoid soft/airy wellness aesthetics.

## Domain Strategy

All of the following were confirmed **available** as of 2026-04-16:

| Domain | Recommended use |
|---|---|
| `zenitweek.com` | Primary — register immediately |
| `zenitweek.cz` | Czech market — register immediately |
| `zenitweek.ai` | AI positioning — register when committing to AI feature |
| `zenitweek.app` | Backup / future redirect |

**Taken:** `zenit.app`, `zenit.ai`, `zen.it` (Italian fintech since 1996), all obvious `.com` variants.

**Strategy:** Register `.com` + `.cz` now as the anchor pair. Add `.ai` when an actual AI feature ships (weekly review summary is the lowest-effort, highest-value candidate). Launching on `.ai` without an AI feature is a promise that needs keeping.

## Key Assumptions to Validate
- [ ] "Zen•it" pun lands visually — test with 5 people outside the project; do they see the split without being told?
- [ ] "Zen" positioning doesn't push the app into the wellness/meditation category — test with target audience (CTOs, VPs)
- [ ] `zenitweek.com` remains available at time of purchase — check before acting

## Not Doing (and Why)
- **`zen.it` domain** — taken since 1996 by Italian fintech, not acquirable
- **`zenit.ai`** — taken (Namecheap); don't chase it
- **Single-word `.com`** (`zenitapp.com`, `usezenit.com`, etc.) — all taken
- **Keeping "week-planner" as the name** — too generic, no brand equity, no story
- **Leaning into AI without a feature** — hollow; damages trust with technical audience

## Open Questions
- What AI feature ships first to justify `zenitweek.ai`? (Weekly review summary via Claude API is the leading candidate)
- Czech tagline: "Zen it, týden po týdnu" — does this feel natural to native speakers?
- Logo execution: who designs the Zen•it typographic split?
