The problem

You're putting sand in first

There's a classic demonstration: fill a jar with sand first, then try to fit in the big rocks — family, health, growth, meaningful work. They don't fit. But reverse it — rocks first — and the sand settles around them naturally.

Most of us plan our weeks like the first jar. Emails, meetings, and small tasks flood in. By Thursday, there's no room left for what we actually care about.
