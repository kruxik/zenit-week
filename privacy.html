Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

1. Controller

Zenit Week is an open-source project operated by Petr Burian, a self-employed individual based in the Czech Republic (part of the EU).
Contact: petr@petrburian.com

2. What data we process and where it lives

Zenit Week has no servers. Your planning data is never transmitted to or stored by us.

  • Local storage — your weekly plans are saved in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device unless you explicitly enable Google Drive sync.
  • Google Drive (optional) — if you sign in with Google, your data is saved to a private, app-specific folder in your own Google Drive using the drive.appdata scope. This folder is invisible to other apps and to us. We never read, copy, or process your Drive data on any server.

3. Google API scopes used

When you choose to sign in with Google, the app requests one scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata — read and write files in the hidden app-data folder of your Google Drive. No access to any other files in your Drive.

Zenit Week's use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — Google Drive sync is strictly opt-in. You can revoke consent at any time by signing out or revoking access in your Google Account settings.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — local browser storage is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app on your device.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third party. The only external service involved is Google Drive, which you control through your own Google Account.

6. Retention and deletion

Local data is retained until you clear your browser storage. Drive data is retained in your Google Drive until you delete it or revoke the app's access. We hold no copies.

7. Your rights under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data, and to object to its processing. Because we hold no data ourselves, these rights are exercised directly through your browser settings (for local storage) or your Google Account (for Drive data). For any questions write to petr@petrburian.com.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Czech Data Protection Authority (ÚOOÚ).

8. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
